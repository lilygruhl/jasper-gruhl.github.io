Stack the sandwich game

The goal of the game is to be a top sandwich maker. You'll need to be quick and accurate. You'll need to be a sandwich wizard of such quality that they could work at Prima Deli.

On the top of the screen are 1 to 10 plates (the more plates the more difficult) with orders above them. The orders will be an ordered list of ingredients like:

  1. slice of bread
  2. tomato
  3. piece of lettuce
  4. 3 slices of ham
  5. slice of bread
On the bottom of the screen will be a conveyor belt of moving ingredients. You can only take the ingredient in front. You will need to use your judgment of where to place the ingredients so you can make all the sandwiches as quickly as possible. If you get an order wrong your turn is over! For example let's say we have the following ingredients on the belt:
Tomato -> Lettuce -> Ham
If you had two orders order #1 calling for ham and then tomato and order #2 calling for ham and then lettuce the player will need to place the ham on order #2 for optimal performance.
